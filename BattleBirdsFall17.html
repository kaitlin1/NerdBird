About Battle Birds

Local circuit to promote competitiveness within the local league community and build a local scene for league all types of league players. There is no barrier to entry, all players are welcome to play. You can come with a pre-made team or our awesome staff will help you find a team to join. We suggest at least one of your players be a San Antonio local so they can rack up the points for our local events.

Battle Birds Rules

+ You must be level 30 and have 16 champions.
+ You must be at least 13 years old to compete.
+ You must not have a misleading nor offensive team name or summoner name.
+ Circuit rules can be found in our comprehensive rule book.
