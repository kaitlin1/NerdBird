Tournament Recap

         For this online tournament we decided to experiment with the Swiss Format in order to give players more games. Ultimately many players, and ourselves, weren't crazy about this format as it didn't have the finality of a bracket stage and many teams simply gave up and quit when they realized they mathematically couldn't place top 4. Despite there being no finals, Nerd Bird legends LP T Pizzle and his duo Chancellor Sama were able to secure their second and first first place finishes respectively.
