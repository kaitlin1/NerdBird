2016 Nerd Bird Recap.

         We started off the year with our Winter WonderLAN, dealt with our first 3-way tie debacle and turned it into a gauntlet challenge. Over all 4 LANs held this year, we've learned from the power issues, internet issues, and server issues. During our most recent LAN, Darude LANstorm, we had to get crafty to deal with the new client and the custom lobby problems. Throughout summer and fall, we were hosting our Friday Night Customs(FNC), where everyone can play and compete for a chance at a mystery skin. So far we've awarded 7 mystery skins and $20 in RP to 9 different players.

         We have tallied up all players who have played, and won in our Nerd Bird events (in-person and online) and put them on our Nerd Bird Leaderboard. TBA. The best pictures of our LAN events are at the bottom of the page. Full albums can be found on our Facebook page.

2017 Upcoming Events

         Based on your feedback, we'll decide what to do for 2017. We can do any myriad of events: meet-ups, online tournaments, Friday night customs, circuit leagues, tournaments. But, we want to know what you guys, the community, want to participate in. If you're interested in helping Nerd Bird with planning or running events, feel free to apply to become a staff member.
