2016 Nerd Bird Recap.

         Looking back on this year, the best part of Nerd Bird has been the community. Each and every one of you contribute to our longevity and without the community (Texas and elsewhere) we could not continue what we do. That being said, we cannot go into 2017 without getting your input on our future tournament and feedback on our previous tournaments. Feedback survey can be found in the left side-bar or below this post.
         This year we've done 4 LAN tournaments, 1 online tournament, and 2 seasons of Friday Night Customs. We started off the year with our Winter WonderLAN, where we dealt with our first 3-way tie debacle and turned it into a gauntlet challenge. During our most recent LAN, Darude LANstorm, we had to get crafty to deal with the new client and the custom lobby problems. But over all 4 LANs held this year, we've learned from the power issues, internet issues, server issues, and most importantly we listened to everyone's feedback and applied what we learned to our tournaments. Besides the LAN tournaments, we held an ambitious 60 person online tournament, the Summer Kickoff (funded by the awesome 'Str8 Rippin'), with the unsuccessful swiss bracket. Even though the Summer Kickoff had its downfalls, plenty of Nerd Bird regulars were able to take home some RP or Steam credit. Another series to mention was our summer and fall Friday Night Customs(FNC), where everyone could play and compete for a chance at a mystery skin. This year we've awarded 7 mystery skins and $20 in RP to 9 different players.

         The staff here at Nerd Bird want to give special thanks to: Dimas 'Str8 Rippin' Gonzales for funding our Summer Kickoff prize pool, Colin 'Conan the Cleric' LaPenotiere for his continued service to Nerd Bird as our head shoutcaster, and our high-school Nerd Birds and their parents who drive to San Antonio to participate in our events!

2017 Upcoming Events

         Based on your feedback, we'll decide what to do for 2017. We can do any myriad of events: meet-ups, online tournaments, Friday night customs, circuit leagues, tournaments. We want to know what you guys, the community, want to participate in, please fill out our feedback survey here. If you're interested in helping Nerd Bird with planning or running events, feel free to apply to become a staff member.

         We have tallied up all players who have played, and won in our Nerd Bird events (in-person and online) and put them on our Nerd Bird Leaderboard(coming soon!). The best pictures of our LAN events are at the bottom of the page. Full albums can be found on our Facebook page.
