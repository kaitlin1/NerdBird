Tournament Recap

         For this tournament we began to broaden our horizons beyond just UTSA students and had players from all of San Antonio and surrounding areas. Once again the eventual tournament winners lost in the first round and managed to go undefeated for the rest of the tournament.
         This was also the second straight tournament that bot lane duo power house, Dangi and Dumbi, were able to go undefeated in the winners bracket up until the finals and then lost 0-2. This tournament was also the first time that Nerd Bird legend Ubiquitous Turnip was able to win it all.
