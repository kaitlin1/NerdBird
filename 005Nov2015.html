7 November 2015

Online Tournament
The VOD is below or here on Youtube.
There are no photos of this online event.
Tournament bracket is below.

Recap: Our November tournament was as challenging as it gets. We had the ambitious goal of running an 80-person/16-team event, and we reached those numbers. The day was marked with technical issues and game delays, but we had an awesome caster duo and some awesome community members who came out to play. Although it was challenging for all parties involved, we all had a good time.

Tournament Information

Game: League of Legends
Venue: Online - NA Server
Date: Saturday, the 7th of November
Teams: Elo-balanced (created and assigned by Nerd Bird)
Format: Round robin into single elimination finals
