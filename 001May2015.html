Tournament Recap

         The tournament to begin them all! With this being our first ever tournament we had thought turnout would be low and had hoped to just get enough people for prize support from Riot. Instead we had to cut off sign ups after 60; we had underestimated how much fervor the 6 month drought in league events had built up.
         This tournament was a cinderalla story as Darude Feedstorm lost their first match and managed to go undefeated for the rest of the tournament from the losers bracket, even 2-0'ing tournament favorites Mozzarella Firefox in the finals, to win the first ever Nerd Bird tournament.
