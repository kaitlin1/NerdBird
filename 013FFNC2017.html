Friday Night Customs - Rule Set

About FNC: For one Friday night each month, we host a Friday Night Custom: 2 elo-balanced teams play in a best of 3. One person from the winning team will be randomly chosen to receive a mystery skin, and all players from the winning team will be entered to win $10 RP.

How to Join: On Friday night, join our Discord server at or before 9PM. Once you're in the Discord lobby, we will invite you into the custom. Once we have enough players to start, we'll balance the teams and play the best of 3. Make sure you can commit to playing 3 games.

Prizes: The mystery skin prize will be awarded at the end of the night. The $10 RP prize will be handed out at the end of the night of the last Friday Night custom. If you win two nights of FNC, your name will be entered twice (or however many times you've won) into the $10 RP giveaway.

Note: Nerd Bird staff are not eligible to receive prizes.
