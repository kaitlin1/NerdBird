Bracket - TBA.

Game: League of Legends
Venue: Online - NA Server
Date: Saturday, 15th of April 2017
Teams: Elo-balanced and premade 5's
Entry: Free
Format: Round robin group stage into bo3 single elimination finals stage.
Rule Book: TBA
Check-In: 9AM CST on our Discord Contact Us: kaitlin@nerd-bird.net or reach out to us on our Discord.

Note: This is a tournament with two bracket options, players have the option of either entering as a solo/duo player in our elo-balanced bracket or enter as a 5-man premade in our premade bracket.

Elo-Balanced Tournament

1st Place: 3-Day BYOC Dreamhack pass for each player.
+ You must sign-up either as a solo player or duo-partners, if you're a duo then each person must sign-up separately and list their duo.
+ Elo-balanced teams will only play against other elo-balanced teams in this tournament.
+ Team assignments will be released: TBA
+ Make sure you add your team captain (assigned by us) within 48-hours of us posting teams, failure to do so will result in us replacing you.
+ Team captains serve as the point-of-contact between admins and players.

Premade-5's Tournament

1st Place: 3-Day BYOC Dreamhack pass for each player.
+ You must sign-up as a full team of 5, incomplete teams will not be put into the bracket.
+ Premade-5 teams will only play against other premade-5 teams in this tournament.
+ Each team must have a team captain for the tournament, they will be the person to contact admins for tournament scores, questions and other issues.
