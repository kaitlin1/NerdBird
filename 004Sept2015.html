Tournament Recap

         Our first LAN event! Despite a few hiccups in power (don't plug everything into the same outlet) and initial issues with the stream, the tournament was able to run relatively smoothly and players had a great time meeting like-minded individuals that were passionate about the same game as themselves. I have to admit we lucked out this tournament because at this time we had no rules in place to deal with 3 way ties in the group stages, so I'd like to thank everyone for losing at least one game each.
         Nerd Bird legend Elitemuffin was able to cement his status as a Nerd Bird legend with his second straight first place finish, earning his title as the most Elite of muffins. This tournament featured many up-and-coming Nerd Bird legends and regulars such as Firefox509, the Shirty's, pspman, and more.
