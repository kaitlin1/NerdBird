The Mission

First and foremost, quality events and dedication to the community are our top priorities.

Nerd Bird Esports is comprised of a small but dedicated group of people, who want to bring the League of Legends community in San Antonio quality tournaments and events.

We strive to give each an every Bird within our community dedicated service, and an even playing field during our events. All of our online or in-person tournaments are elo-balanced so that on average each team is the same rank/mmr/elo.

We want players of all skill levels, of all roles, and of all ages to feel welcomed in the Nerd Bird community. We also want Nerd Bird volunteers who share that same mission. If you're interested in becoming a tournament admin, shoutcaster or another position (something we don't know exists), feel free to submit an application!
